Explore Wildlife in Odisha
Simlipal National Park
Mayurbhanj
Famous for its rich biodiversity including tigers and elephants.
Bhitarkanika National Park
Kendrapara
Known for its mangrove forests and saltwater crocodiles.
Chilika Wildlife Sanctuary
Ganjam
A haven for migratory birds and the largest coastal lagoon in India.
Nandankanan Zoological Park
Bhubaneswar
A zoological park with a variety of species including white tigers, and an ecosystem of flora and fauna.
Debrigarh Wildlife Sanctuary
Sambalpur
Known for its rich wildlife and scenic beauty, it's a perfect spot for nature lovers.
Karlapat Wildlife Sanctuary
Kalahandi
A hidden gem, known for its tranquil atmosphere and rich biodiversity, including leopards and wild boars.
Satkosia Tiger Reserve
Angul
A tiger reserve known for its dense forests and river views, home to a variety of wild species, including tigers.
