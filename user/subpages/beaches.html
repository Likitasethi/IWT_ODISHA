Beautiful Beaches of Odisha

Puri Beach
Puri
Famous golden sand beach with religious significance. Perfect for watching sunrise and cultural activities.
Chandipur Beach
Balasore
Unique beach where the sea recedes up to 5 km during low tide. Known as the hide-and-seek beach.
Gopalpur Beach
Ganjam
Pristine beach with colonial charm. Perfect for water sports and watching fishing boats.
Konark Beach
Konark
Beautiful beach near the famous Sun Temple. Known for its scenic beauty and clean shores.
Astaranga Beach
Puri
Famous for spectacular sunsets and fishing activities. Name means 'colorful sunset' in Odia.
Ramchandi Beach
Konark
Serene and quiet beach, perfect for a peaceful retreat. Known for its temple and lush surroundings.
